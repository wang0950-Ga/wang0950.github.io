About Me

I think it is inevitable that people will be surrounded by feeling of depression and hopeless for any unpredictable reason. My solution is trying to recall the most beautiful and warmest moment in my life, and to feel every detail that make me happiness. After I use this to resist a wave of depression, I will dive into a long-term plan, such as, to prepare for important exam, to learn another language, etc. When I can not concentrate anymore, I just do the things I like. For example, play videogame, watch movie and so on. When I feel hungry, I go to cook. When I feel sleepy, I go to sleep. When I am confused about life, I ask myself what I really want in my deep inside. When I can not find the answer but I have to move on, I go to the Bible.

After Marth 17th 2020 , I have done a bunch of things I never thought. I pass the postgraduate examination of China. I received an acceptant letter for a master degree from a fancy university in China. I find my job during lockdown. I got a certification of Python3 course. I pass the IELTS test overall 6.5. And now, I am here, Ottawa, try my best to deal with my homework.

Don't make life complicated. - by myself
